## 10. CV Copy Standards

When writing or updating CV content, apply these rules without exception:

- Reverse chronological order, role first
- No superlatives, no filler phrases, no marketing language
- Include organisational context (scale, sector, complexity) — it carries more weight than adjectives
- Include team sizes where known (they are documented above per role)
- Frame for CIO-level readership: lead with strategic and governance scope, substantiate with technical specifics
- Academic section is prominent — it is a differentiator, not a credential list item
- Certifications listed with issuing body only; no expiry dates
